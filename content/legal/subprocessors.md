CanvasBuddy uses the third-party service providers below ("subprocessors") to operate the Service. Each processes personal data on our behalf and under its own terms and security commitments. This page is provided for transparency and complements our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

| Subprocessor | Purpose | Personal data processed | Primary region |
|---|---|---|---|
| Supabase | Authentication and database hosting | Account email, hashed password, Canvas credentials (base URL, access/refresh tokens), user preferences | United States |
| Brevo | Transactional email (sign-up confirmation, password reset) | Email address | EU |
| Anthropic | AI study assistant (Claude API) | Your assistant questions and derived Canvas data — never your Canvas token | US |
| Instructure / Canvas | Source of your academic data via your authorized connection | Data you authorize us to read from your Canvas account | Depends on your school |
| Vercel | Application hosting and content delivery | Request metadata (e.g. IP address, user agent) | United States |

We do not sell personal data or share it with advertisers. Anthropic does not use API data to train its models by default under its commercial terms.

We may update this list as our providers change. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date, consistent with our commitment not to make retroactive changes that reduce your protections.
